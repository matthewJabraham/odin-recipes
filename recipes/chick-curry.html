Chicken Curry Recipe

This recipe will help you create an absolutely beautiful chicken curry, all in under an hour. Get your fork ready and dig in to this amazing recipe!

Ingredient

  • Sunflower oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Ginger
  • Chicken thighs
  • Tikka seasoning
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Greek yoghurt
  • Coriander
  • Ground almonds
  • Naan bread

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a casserole dish or large frying pan
  2. Add onion and salt and fry for 10mins
  3. Add garlic and ginger once onion is cooked
  4. Chock chick into 3cm pieces, add to pan and fry for 5 mins before stirring through the spice past and tomatoes, along with 205ml water.
  5. Bring to the boil and simmer for 25-30 mins
  6. Stir through the yoghurt, coriander and ground almonds, season and serve with warm naan bread
