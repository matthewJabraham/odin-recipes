Margherita Pizza Recipe

This recipe will list for you the ingredients to create your very own Margherita pizza! Make sure you also follow all of the steps listed out closely to ensure you create the best possible pizza

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Granulated sugar
  • Active dry yeast
  • Kosher salt
  • Water
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Parmigiano-reggiano cheese
  • Fresh mozzarella cheeese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Your choice of tomato based pizza sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Scrape the dough onto a well-floured counter top and knead the dough for 3 minutes
  3. Cover the dough with a kitchen towel and allow to rise in a warm dry area of the kitchen for 2hrs
  4. Preheat oven and pizza steal or stone
  5. As the oven is preheating, assemble the ingredients
  6. Seperate the dough into two equal-sized portions
  7. Assemble the pizza
  8. Drizzle or brush the dough lightly (using your fingertips) with olive oil
