Crispy Shredded Chicken Recipe

This recipe will help you to create a succulant crispy shredded chicken meal. Make sure to follow all of the steps carefully and ensure you buy the correct ignredients to make this the best tasting meal possible!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Red pepper
  • Vegetbale oil
  • Green pepper
  • Cornflour
  • Chilli sauce
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • Sesame oil
  • Spring onions

Steps

  1. Slice the chicken into strips
  2. Meanwhile, add oil to the wok and cook peppers for 3-4 minutes
  3. Spread cornflour in a shallow bowl. Season, then add marinate chicken
  4. Fill wok with oil to about 0.5cm deep, then heat to medium/high
  5. Carefully add chicken cooking for 3-4 mins
  6. Mix together the remaiing soy, sweet chilli, garlic past and sesame oil in a large bowl. Add everything togetehr and you're done!
